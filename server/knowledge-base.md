## 3) Policies

### Deposit Policy
- **Amount:** $50
- **Purpose:** Reduce no-shows and reserve provider time.
- **How it works:**
  - A $50 deposit is collected at scheduling to hold your appointment.
  - The deposit is applied to your onboarding/initial visit fee or first month membership if you enroll.
  - If you cancel or reschedule with at least 24 hours notice, the deposit can be transferred to a new appointment time.
  - If you cancel with less than 24 hours notice or do not show, the deposit is forfeited.

### Membership Billing
- **Billing frequency:** monthly
- **Auto-renew:** True
- **Default minimum term:** 3 months
- **Cancellation:**
  - Members can request cancellation any time unless a minimum term applies.
  - Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period (no partial-month refunds).
- **Refunds:**
  - Membership fees are generally non-refundable once the month begins, because provider time and support are reserved.

### No-Show Policy
- **Initial visit:** Deposit is forfeited if no-show or late cancellation (<24 hours).
- **Follow-up visits:** May be charged a missed-visit fee or require a deposit for rescheduling.

- **Q:** Is menopausal hormone therapy safe?
  **A:** In November 2025, the FDA removed the black-box warning for menopausal hormone therapy.
